Indian Birds

peacock

A peacock use its ornate train in a courtship display, wherein it raises the tail feathers into an arched fan and quivers them. The wings are held half open and drooped and it periodically vibrates the long feathers, producing a ruffling sound. It faces the peahen initially and might turn around to display the tail. The peacock may also freeze over food to invite a peahen in a form of courtship feeding.
